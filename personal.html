Movies

One of my favorite pastimes is watching Indian movies. I watch a TON of them, and I could talk for hours about certain ones. I am always open to recommendations, so reach out!

I think Malayalam movies are my favorite, probably because I grew up watching them, but also I find the acting and stories excellent. However, I am convinced that I know all the Indian languages, and subtitles exist, so I pretty much watch all languages.

Instead of Letterboxed or anything seriously involed, everytime I watch a movie, I want to describe it in 5 words and rate it. As a heads up, I do not care if your favorite actor's stunt double did a backflip or for action movies in general.

  • Pet Detective. Malayalam. 8.5/10. Komali, Well-paced, Entertaining, Witty, Slapstick.
  • The Girlfriend. Telegu. 9.2/10. Colorful, Emotional, Slice-of-life, Intentional, Thought-Provoking.
