Movies

One of my favorite pastimes is watching Indian movies. I watch a TON of them, and I could talk for hours about certain ones. I am always open to recommendations, so reach out!

I think Malayalam movies are my favorite, probably because I grew up watching them, but also I find the acting and stories excellent. However, I am convinced that I know all the Indian languages, and subtitles exist, so I pretty much watch all languages.

Instead of Letterboxed or anything seriously involed, everytime I watch a movie, I want to describe it in a few sentences and rate it. As a heads up, I do not care if your favorite actor's stunt double did a backflip or for action movies in general, and this influences my ratings of course. I also am too much of a scaredy cat to watch horror movies.

Ratings Scale: 10/10 being the best, 1/10 being the worst. 5/10 is average.

  • Ponman. Malayalam. 10/10. This is a fast-paced engaging film about middle-class lives, and is quite thought provoking. I found myself enjoying action scenes when I usually do not, and I think this is one of Basil Joseph's best performances.
  • Kannathil Muthamittal. Tamil. 9.9/10. This is one of my favorite Mani Ratnam x ARR films, and I am continuously amazed by the storytelling, soundtrack, and cinematography. I definitely recommend grabbing tissues. In particular, this movie feels like a poignant reminder of a mother-daughter relationship.
  • Pet Detective. Malayalam. 8.5/10. Another komali movie, perfect for a family entertainer. It was not too long and well paced, but the story was a little predictable.
  • The Girlfriend. Telegu. 9.2/10. The color grading and visuals and costuming was excellent. I think the story was a little dramatic in some places, but I understand why this was necessary to communicate the message in an articulate manner. I really liked the songs as well, and the ending was good.
